About Myself!

Hello, I'm Janmejay, a Machine Learning Engineer with expertise in Python, Data Analysis, and Data Visualization.

I hold a Master's in Machine Learning from Stevens Institute of Technology in New Jersey, where I honed my skills in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Big Data Technologies. Outside of work, I'm passionate about Data Visualization, believing that a picture can speak volumes.

My journey in the field began as a Research and Development Engineer at Research Center Imarat, where I contributed to digital signature algorithm research and website development. Later, I gained hands-on experience as a Machine Learning Intern at Tech Explica, working on automation and data analysis projects. I've also served as a Teaching Assistant at Princeton University, helping students apply ML knowledge to real-world scenarios and collaborating with peers from MIT.
